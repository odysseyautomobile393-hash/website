Professional Paint Correction & Polishing

Your vehicle's paintwork is exposed to harsh environmental elements daily—UV rays, bird droppings, tree sap, acid rain, and more. Over time, this causes swirl marks, oxidation, and dull finish. Our professional cut and polish service restores your vehicle's original shine and protection.

What is Cut & Polish?

Cut and polish is a paint correction process that removes imperfections from your vehicle's clear coat. The "cut" (or cutting) removes the contaminated outer layer using specialized abrasive compounds, while the "polish" refines the surface to a brilliant shine. This process restores clarity and depth to your paint.

Our Process

  1. Thorough vehicle washing and drying
  2. Paint depth measurement and assessment
  3. Clay bar treatment to remove embedded contaminants
  4. Machine polishing with appropriate compound selection
  5. Multiple stage buffing and polishing
  6. Final inspection and touch-ups as needed
  7. Application of protective sealant or ceramic coating (optional)

What Can Be Corrected?

  • Swirl marks and fine scratches
  • Paint oxidation and fading
  • Water spots and contamination
  • Hologram marks from previous polishing
  • Minor paint runs and overspray
  • Environmental etching
  • Dull or hazy clear coat
