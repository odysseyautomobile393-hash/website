Turning Wrecks into Opportunities

Do you have a wrecked, damaged, or salvage vehicle that you'd like to sell? At Salvage Odyssey Auto, we purchase wrecked cars in any condition. Whether your vehicle has been in a serious accident, suffered major damage, or simply isn't worth repairing, we can help.

Fair Pricing

We offer competitive prices for wrecked vehicles based on make, model, condition, and salvage value.

Any Condition

We accept vehicles in any condition - totaled, damaged, non-running, or just unwanted.

Quick Process

Fast assessment and payment. We handle the paperwork and logistics for you.

Parts Sourcing

We salvage and recycle components for ongoing use, minimizing waste.

We Can Help With:

  • Vehicles damaged in collisions or accidents
  • Cars with major mechanical failures
  • Vehicles with water damage or flood damage
  • Fire-damaged vehicles
  • Non-running or abandoned vehicles
  • Vehicles with significant wear and tear
  • Insurance write-offs and total losses

The Process

  1. Submit your vehicle information through the form below
  2. We review the details and provide an initial assessment
  3. Schedule an inspection at our location or on your property
  4. Receive our offer and detailed valuation
  5. Accept the offer and arrange payment
  6. We handle towing and all necessary paperwork
