Professional Spoiler Installation

A spoiler is more than just a style upgrade—it's an aerodynamic enhancement that reduces lift at high speeds, improving vehicle stability, traction, and handling. At Salvage Odyssey Auto, we specialize in professional spoiler installation for all vehicle types.

Spoiler Types Available

  • Lip Spoilers - Subtle, sleek enhancement for a modern look
  • Wing Spoilers - Aggressive, high-performance design
  • GT Spoilers - Perfect for sports cars and performance vehicles
  • Roof Spoilers - Factory-style enhancement
  • Deck Spoilers - Custom deck lid or boot designs
  • Custom Designs - Tailored to your specific vehicle and preferences

The Installation Process

  1. Vehicle inspection and surface assessment
  2. Surface preparation and cleaning
  3. Precise measurement and marking
  4. Spoiler installation with secure fastening
  5. Alignment and fit verification
  6. Paint protection and sealing (if applicable)
  7. Final quality inspection

Benefits of Adding a Spoiler

Improved Aerodynamics

Reduces lift, improves downforce, and enhances stability

Enhanced Performance

Better handling and traction at high speeds

Stylish Appearance

Transforms your vehicle's visual presence

Increased Resale Value

Quality modifications can enhance vehicle value
